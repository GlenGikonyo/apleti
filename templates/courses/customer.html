Customer Relations, Care and Professionalism at the Workplace

Objective of the course

The goal of this course is to lead participants to: Understand, apply and to discover that exceptional customer service skills provide employees with new insights into customer behaviour, and effective tools for creating lasting customer satisfaction and be strategically positioned for administrative excellence. The course also aims at helping the participants to identify their misconceptions about assertiveness and practice best assertiveness skills; and address the emotional and cognitive roadblocks that impede their ability to be assertive at their workplace.

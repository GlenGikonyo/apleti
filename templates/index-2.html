About us

African Prime Leadership Training Institute (APLETI) exists to develop and create impactful empowered professional leaders with changed mindsets, impacting their organizations and communities positively. APLETI offers empowerment leadership programs for established and emerging organizational leaders with global changing mindsets.

As an Institute we endeavor to meet the urgency of adopting impact-oriented leadership through: Practices leading to professional leadership practiced in organizations at different levels; Coordinated efforts from Governments to strengthen policies and enhance implementation; Civil society, to raise awareness and advocate for impactful leadership styles; Academia, to improve education and training for an integrated- resilient workforce; and Private sector, to invest in advanced sustainable technologies.

Mission

To Produce Empowered Leaders with Transformed Mindsets and Integrity through Training, Mentorship and Coaching for Global Service.

Vision

Mentoring The Next Generation of Empowered Leaders of Integrity Globally.

Goal

Creating an Empowered Generation of Responsible Leaders with Transformed Mindsets & Integrity, Providing Services Globally.

Motto

Leadership With Integrity and Changed Mindsets.

Our Values

Our values guide us to act with integrity, foster innovation, and build trust, creating a positive impact on those we serve and our community.

Professionalism

APLETI strives to bring up high-impact professional leaders who are results-oriented.

Integrity

APLETI envisions producing world leaders with high levels of integrity and ethics.

Teamwork

APLETI respects the collaborative style of leadership that exemplifies teamwork at all levels of organizational leadership and management.

Partnership

APLETI embraces partnerships with like-minded institutions and organizations at local, national, regional, and global levels.

Respect for Humanity

APLETI respects the sanctity of human life and the value of humanity as created by God in his image.

Strategic Objectives & Overall Outcomes

Our strategic objectives and outcomes guide our mission to develop impactful leaders globally

Strategic Objectives

  1. 1.

    To provide professional technical human resources to support the training programs and consultancy services.

  2. 2.

    To create a team of reliable trainers to lead the facilitation of high impact leadership empowerment programs and consultancy services.

  3. 3.

    To develop programs and projects, various consultancy services and relevant policies and procedures governing their implementation.

  4. 4.

    To develop and source for strategic partnerships with international organizations, government, non-governmental organizations, churches, and like-minded institutions.

  5. 5.

    To develop contextual entrepreneurial activities that helps the Institute to maintain its competitive advantage in Africa and the Global market.

  6. 6.

    To develop grant attracting fundraising strategies to create sustainable financial resources base to support training programs and consultancy services.

Overall Outcomes

  1. 1.

    Produce Ethical Leaders with integrity in the delivery of their services to the community and the wider Society.

  2. 2.

    Create a crop of high impact leaders whose abilities and capacities have been enhanced to act as change agents in changing their societies.

  3. 3.

    Develop Leaders who will become coaches and mentors for the future generation of leaders.

  4. 4.

    Enhance international collaboration and partnerships that results to mutual interdependence between local, national, and international organizations.

  5. 5.

    Offer professional programs and projects, various consultancy services and relevant policies and procedures governing their implementation.

Founder/Executive Director

Rev. Dr. Zablon B. Mutongu

The Rev. Dr. Zablon B. Mutongu holds a PhD in Organization Development and a Doctorate in Organizational Development and Transformation (DODT) from Cebu Doctors' University, Philippines; A Graduate Diploma in Community Development & Organization Management, from Coady International Institute, St. Francis Xavier University, Canada; A Masters Degree in Theology (MTh.) from Aberdeen University, Scotland; A Bachelor of Divinity Degree (BD) from St. University, Kenya; A Diploma in Theology; and A Certificate in Agriculture from Bukura Agricultural College, Kenya. He also holds several certified training Certificates in Leadership, Management, Pension Fund Management, Training of Trainers, Mentorship and Coaching, Peacebuilding and Conflict Transformation, Monitoring and Evaluation, International Labour Organization (ILO) Certification, and Many others.

Dr. Zablon Mutongu has worked in different fields including Agriculture, High School Technical Teacher, Church Ministry, Middle Level Training Institutions, Higher Level Education Colleges, culminating to being a University Lecturer, Senior Lecturer, Head of Department, Director of University Enterprise Unit and finally becoming the Deputy Vice Chancellor, Finance & Administration. This has given him invariable experiences. He also enjoys an international exposure having been a regional and international Board Member in various organizations.
